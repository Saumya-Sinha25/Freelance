About Our Company

Kailash Chandra Aggarwal
(Founder)
K.C. Enterprises, a leader in the Dhoop industry, was founded in 2004, but our legacy dates back to 1973 with Shri Kailash Chandra Aggarwal. Today, under the leadership of Mr. Rajeev Aggarwal, we have established a respected presence in the market. We specialize in an extensive range of high-quality products, including various types of Dhoops, cones, 0% wax-free ghee diyas, and sambrani cups, all crafted with a commitment to excellence.
Understanding the significance of religious rituals, we provide essential Puja Samagri that enhances worship experiences. Our diverse offerings ensure that every devotion is complemented with the right products, fostering a deeper connection with the divine. K.C. Enterprises is dedicated to meeting customer demands while maintaining the highest quality standards, making us a trusted partner for spiritual needs.
In addition to our core products, we are continually expanding our catalog to include innovative items that reflect contemporary preferences while honoring traditional values. Our focus on sustainability and ethical sourcing further underscores our commitment to quality and community. As we continue to innovate and expand, our mission remains focused on enriching the lives of our customers through quality and tradition.
